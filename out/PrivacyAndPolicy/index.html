We respect your privacy.
This Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) applies to all of our apps, services, features, software, and website (collectively “Services”), unless specified otherwise. This Privacy Policy helps to explain our information (including message) practices.
This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and forms part of the KeyHelper Terms of Service (“Terms”) that you have agreed to in order to use KeyHelper. Any terms used in this Privacy Policy will have the same meaning as the equivalent defined terms in the KeyHelper Terms of Service, unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy or the context requires otherwise. 1. Information we Collect

KeyHelper receives or collects information when we operate and provide our Services, including when you install, access, or use our Services.
1.1 Information you provide
(i) Your Account Information: You provide your email address to create a KeyHelper account. You provide us the email in your email address book on a regular basis, including those of both the users of our Services and your other contacts. You confirm you are authorised to provide us such emails. You may also add other information to your account, such as a profile name, profile picture, and status message.
(ii) Your Messages: We will retain your messages for a period of 365 days in the ordinary course of providing our Services to you (“Retention Period”). Once your messages (including your chats, photos, videos, voice messages, files) are delivered, they will be retained for the Retention Period. After the Retention Period, your messages will be deleted unless such messages are stored on our servers for legal and/or business purposes in compliance with applicable laws.
(iii) Your Connections: To help you organise how you communicate with others, you can create, join or get added to groups, and such groups get associated with your account information.
(iv) Customer Support: You may provide us with information related to your use of our Services, including copies of your messages, and how to contact you so we can provide you customer support. For example, you may send us an email with information relating to our app performance or other issues.
1.2 Automatically Collected Information
(i) Usage and Log Information: We collect service-related, diagnostic, and performance information. This includes information about your activity (such as how you use our Services, how you interact with others using our Services, and the like), log files, and diagnostic, crash, website, and performance logs and reports.
(ii) Device and Connection Information: We collect device-specific information when you install, access, or use our Services. This includes information such as hardware model, operating system information, browser information, IP address, mobile network information including phone number, and device identifiers.
(iii) Cookies: We use cookies to operate and provide our Services, including to provide our Services that are web-based (if any), improve your experiences, understand how our Services are being used, and customise our Services. For example, we may use cookies to provide KeyHelper for web and desktop and other web-based services (if any). We may also use cookies to understand which of our FAQs are most popular and to show you relevant content related to our Services. Additionally, we may use cookies to remember your choices, such as your language preferences, and otherwise to customise our Services for you.
(iv) Status Information: We collect information about your online and status message changes on our Services, such as whether you are online (your “online status”), when you last used our Services (your “last seen status”), and when you last updated your status message.
1.3 Third-Party Information
(i) Information Others Provide About you: We receive information other people provide us, which may include information about you. For example, when other users you know use our Services, they may provide email from their email address book (just as you may provide theirs), or they may send you a message, send messages to groups to which you belong.
(ii) Third-Party Providers: We work with third-party providers to help us operate, provide, improve, understand, customise, support, and market our Services. For example, we work with companies to distribute our apps, provide our infrastructure, delivery, and other systems, supply map and places information, help us understand how people use our Services, and market our Services. These providers may provide us information about you in certain circumstances; for example, app stores may provide us reports to help us diagnose and fix service issues.
(iii) Third-Party Services: We allow you to use our Services in connection with third-party services. If you use our Services with such third-party services, we may receive information about you from them; for example, if you use the KeyHelper share button on a news service to share a news article with your KeyHelper contacts or groups, or if you choose to access our Services through a mobile carrier’s or device provider’s promotion of our Services. Please note that when you use third-party services, their own terms and privacy policies will govern your use of those services.
